About SnackTrack

Who We Are

Welcome to SnackTrack, your ultimate destination for a seamless and delightful canteen experience. We aim to connect students, faculty, and staff with delicious snacks, beverages, and meals, all from the comfort of their devices.

Our mission is to streamline the canteen experience, providing features like real-time order tracking, user-friendly profiles, and a fast, efficient ordering system.


Our Mission

At SnackTrack, we believe in making everyday moments more enjoyable. Our goal is to empower students and staff with a quick and effortless way to order their favorite snacks and meals, fostering a sense of community and connection in the canteen.

We strive for excellence by continuously enhancing our platform to meet your needs and exceed your expectations.
